Moneii Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Moneii collects account details, profile preferences, and financial entries required to provide budgeting, analytics, voice logging, and AI features.

Data Collected

  • Account info: email and authentication metadata
  • Profile info: display name, avatar (optional), currency preference
  • Financial data: expenses, income, transfers, and account data
  • Voice data: recorded audio used for transcription
  • AI usage data: prompts and responses for in-app assistant features

How Data Is Used

  • Operate app features and personalization
  • Provide analytics and insights
  • Support premium features and usage limits
  • Maintain account security and service reliability

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and storage
  • OpenAI APIs for transcription and AI responses
  • RevenueCat and app stores for subscriptions

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account in-app via Profile > Delete Account. Deletion removes the authentication account and associated app data.

Contact

Use the support email listed in the app store listing for privacy requests.
