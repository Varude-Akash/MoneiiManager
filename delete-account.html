Moneii Account Deletion

Last updated: February 27, 2026

You can request deletion of your Moneii account and associated data directly inside the app.

How To Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Moneii app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Authentication account tied to your login
  • Profile details stored by the app
  • Expense and financial records linked to your account
  • AI and voice usage records linked to your account

Data Retention

Account-linked app data is queued for deletion when the request is confirmed. Some limited operational logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Need Help

If you cannot access your account, contact support using the email listed on the app store page and include your registered email address.
