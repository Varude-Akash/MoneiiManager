# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

MoneiiManager collects account details, profile preferences, and financial entries required to provide budgeting, analytics, and AI features.

## Data Collected

- Account info: email and authentication metadata
- Profile info: display name, avatar (optional), currency preference
- Financial data: expenses, income, transfers, account data
- Voice data: recorded audio used for transcription
- AI usage data: prompts and responses for in-app assistant features

## How Data Is Used

- To operate app features and personalization
- To provide analytics and insights
- To support premium features and usage limits
- To maintain account security and service reliability

## Third-Party Services

- Supabase for authentication, database, and storage
- OpenAI APIs for transcription and AI responses

## Account Deletion

Users can delete their account in-app via:

- Profile > Delete Account

Deletion removes authentication account and associated app data.

## Contact

Use the support email listed in the app store listing for privacy requests.
